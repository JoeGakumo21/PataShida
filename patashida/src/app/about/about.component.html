About Us

Patashida Web Application takes advantage of machine learning principles to predict the plant disease from user input.
The farmer is required to upload a picture of the plant then he/she will be able to receive the possible disease the plant is most likely to have
The farmer is also able to receive a short description or text of steps to follow in solving the issue.


This PataShida Web Application mainly aims in solving several issues that farmers undergo.
